Welcome

Doyah is a fast, fun and easy way to spontaneously hang out with your friends. Doyah is a Hyper Island student project and is not a real product. It's a prototype that we want to get founding for so we can actually develop it.

Effective planning

Send a Doyah in three easy steps.

Guaranteed response

Your friends have 30 min to reply.

Enjoy together

Stop wasting time and enjoy life!

1.Communicate with emojis

With Doyah you communicate with emojis and tags. Pick an emoji from our library that illustrate which activity you want to do with your friends. Then choose a time and place for when and where to meet up.

2.Send to friends

You can send a Doyah to more than one of your contacts. It is possible to group your contact to get a better overview of your contacts and to make it more efficient.

3.Fast reply

When you receive a Doyah you have 30 minutes to reply yes or no. You have time to check your calendar and there are no need to write a long excuse for why you can't meet. When you answer the chat function will be avaiable.

The apple watch

We see that smart devices such as the Apple watch is getting more and more popular. The usage of smart watches and werables is expanding and we want Doyah to be aviable for what would come in the future. Doyah is design for devices where you can't interact as much as you do with your phone.
